03 : Selected Projects see all →

  • SemanticSentry

    2025 · library

    Open-source library accompanying our BlackboxNLP submission. A model-agnostic framework for detecting semantic drift in embedding spaces — quantifying geometric transformations induced by fine-tuning, quantization, and LoRA updates without requiring labeled evaluation data. Combines CKA for global structure, NPS for local neighborhoods, and Isotropy for spectral geometry, accelerated by FAISS nearest-neighbor search and surfaced through W&B and MLflow hooks.

  • AdLovin

    Oct 2025 · CalHacks-12 winner

    A multi-agent advertisement analysis pipeline combining computer vision, vector retrieval, and LLM reasoning to extract marketing and business signals from video. Reaches 70–90% frame deduplication and reduces vision-API cost by roughly 70% while preserving downstream extraction quality.
